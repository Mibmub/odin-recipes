Wok chicken

Description

This recipe is perfect for when you have little time and just want something quickly, yet healthy to eat. It's a combination of a few ingredients that blend well togheter and create a well-balanced meal, covering all the necessities. You can add as much extra's as you want, making this recipe compatble in every situation.

ingredients

  • Noodles
  • (frozen)Wok-vegatbles
  • Wok-sauce
  • marinated chicken
  • olive oil

    • optional extra's

  • Nuts
  • pine nuts
  • Basil
  • cherry tomatoes
    •

Steps

  1. Start with putting some olive oil in you pan.
  2. Add the chicken and stur reguraly.
  3. Add the vegatbles let it steam.
  4. While stiring, add the wok sauce.
  5. Finally add the noodles and slowly mix.

    6. Serving

  6. Serve with nuts, basil and/or cherry tomatoes.
